Operating Systems (WS2324)

 

The main task of an operating system is the controlling, management and distribution of resources of a computer system to the users. Resources are CPUs, main memory, files, input/output devices, network services, and in general all resources, which are connected to the computer system. In addition, an operating system provides the user interface for controlling the system as well as protection mechanisms, tools, libraries and development tools.

The objective of this course is to discuss the concepts, which are implemented by classic and modern operating systems.

The course (lectures, exercises and exam) is held in English language. All slide sets and exercise sheets are available in English and in German language. The content of the English and German slide sets and exercise sheets is identical.

The slide sets are the basis for the third edition of the German-language book Betriebssysteme kompakt, which was published in June 2022 by Springer Vieweg. ISBN: 978-3-662-64717-2

Also, a bilingual (English-German) edition exists - Operating Systems / Betriebssysteme, which was published in July 2023 (second edition) by Springer Vieweg. ISBN: 978-3-658-42229-5

Organizational information about this course

This website provides information about the course, and it offers the complete lecture material.

For every slide set, there is an exercise sheet that repeats the relevant content of the slide set. You can solve the exercise sheets by yourself using the slide sets. For some exercise questions, you need to do some research on your own. For verification, the sample solutions of the exercise sheets are online.

In the exercise sessions, I will assist all participants and groups if they have any questions. There will be no simple recitation or copying of the solutions on the whiteboard because, from my experience, this is not very effective.

The final exam at the end of the semester is a 90-minute written exam. You can find exams from previous semesters and the corresponding sample solutions on this page. Relevant for the exam are the contents of the slide sets that were discussed during the semester and, thus, first of all, the corresponding exercise sheets. There will be questions from every exercise sheet in the exam.

If you have any questions or if you found an error in the course material, don't hesitate to get in touch with me personally or by email.
