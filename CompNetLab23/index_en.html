Schedule of the Course

Lab assignment organisation

Lab sessions take place on Friday between 10:00 and 15:45 in Room 1-237.

The course I4b is split into three groups, and each group gets access to the lab for the entire time slot (10:00-15:45) every three weeks.

The reason to do this is that you cannot solve a lab exercise sheet in 90 minutes, and you cannot save your configuration on the workstations.

 

  • Group 1 of I4b has the lab on the following days: 14.04.2023, 05.05.2023, 25.05.2023, 16.06.2023
  • Group 2 of I4b has the lab on the following days: 21.04.2023, 12.05.2023, 02.06.2023, 23.06.2023
  • Group 3 of I4b has the lab on the following days: 28.04.2023, 19.05.2023, 09.06.2023, 30.06.2023
