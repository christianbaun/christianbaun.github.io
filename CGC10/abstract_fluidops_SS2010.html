Managing heterogeneous hardware resources to provide a compute cloud for enterprise applications

We present the eCloudManager management platform which leverages semantic integration technology to integrate and automate all layers (storage, network, compute and applications) in the datacenter. The integration can be achieved vertically from hardware to applications and horizontally, for instance by supporting multiple compute platforms such as VMware, public clouds and non virtualized servers. We present two eCloud use cases: SAP Landscape as a Service and an IaaS solution based on raw blade servers.
